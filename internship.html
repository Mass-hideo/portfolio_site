Mediaocean Internship

June 3rd - August 2nd, 2024

This summer, I had the privilege of working at Mediaocean for two months, as an intern in the User Experience department. While there, I had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects. The first one, that lasted across the entire internship, involved comparing components from Mediaocean's design system across their platforms, and finding potential use cases for designs from other companies.


The second project involved updating a component design in figma that had never been implemented, and then creating documentation for said component.


The final project was the most intensive. It's crux of it was collaborating with my department manager in designing a contact list. It involved meeting with the clients who had made the request, creating a preliminary design, taking feedback from my manager, and then working to create a refined design that could potentially be eventually implemented in Mediaocean's Prisma Omni platform.
